MAD stands for Mission Azaadi Document. The Mission Azaadi Document made an effort to systematically capture strategies of politics, bureaucracy, education and police by ways of consultation, dialogue, argument and evaluation. Mission Azaadi Document was developed as an overarching advocacy manual for the LGBT community that clearly defines objectives, strategies and activities as developed by community persons. The Mission Azaadi Document is an open document that can be used as a reference for various individuals and organisations working towards reading down Section 377. The ultimate Goal of Sexual minorities attaining equality, freedom and right to live with dignity in India. The purpose of the document is to serve as a reference manual to LGBTQH communities across India to conduct their advocacy activities. Individuals, collectives, organisations can evaluate each strategy and activity to choose, customise the same to their lived realities. The manual serves as a tool to build community initiatives in new areas and locations. It also provides possible methods of raising the resources for conducting advocacy programs and sustaining them.
